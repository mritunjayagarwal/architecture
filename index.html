About AR & Associates

Founded in 2020 by AR. Anubhav Rastogi, AR & Associates is a premier architectural firm specializing in innovative design solutions that blend contemporary excellence with functional practicality. Our team brings together diverse expertise from leading architecture firms across Lucknow, Noida, and Roorkee.

We are known for our approachable nature and clarity in design thinking, excelling at turning complex challenges into simple, elegant solutions. AR & Associates has earned a reputation for thoughtful, user-centric designs and strong client relationships across residential, commercial, and institutional projects.

Our Signature Projects

From luxury hotels to sustainable office complexes, our portfolio showcases the diversity and excellence of Indian architecture. Each project reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and cultural sensitivity.

Our projects span across major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Hyderabad. We have successfully completed over 500 projects ranging from residential villas to large-scale commercial developments, always maintaining the highest standards of design and construction quality.
