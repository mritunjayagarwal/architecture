About Vastu Architects

India's premier architectural firm blending traditional Vastu principles with modern design innovation. We create spaces that harmonize with nature while meeting contemporary needs.

Founded in 1995, Vastu Architects has been at the forefront of sustainable architecture in India. Our team of experienced architects combines ancient Indian architectural wisdom with cutting-edge technology to create buildings that are both beautiful and functional.

We specialize in residential, commercial, and institutional projects across India, with a particular focus on eco-friendly designs that respect our cultural heritage while embracing modern innovation.

Our Signature Projects

From luxury hotels to sustainable office complexes, our portfolio showcases the diversity and excellence of Indian architecture. Each project reflects our commitment to quality, innovation, and cultural sensitivity.

Our projects span across major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Hyderabad. We have successfully completed over 500 projects ranging from residential villas to large-scale commercial developments, always maintaining the highest standards of design and construction quality.
