About ME

A graduate of the Uttra Pradesh technical university (2012), Ar. Anubhav Rastogi gained valuable experience working with leading architecture firms in Lucknow, noida and Roorkee. In 2020, driven by a passion to create meaningful and innovative spaces, he founded AR & Associates.

Known for his approachable nature and clarity in design thinking, he excels at turning complex challenges into simple, elegant solutions. Under his leadership AR & Associates, has earned a reputation for thoughtful, user-centric designs and strong client relationships

AR & Associates

Is a vibrant and design-focused studio driven by a passion for creating spaces that are not only unique and functional but also affordable. Our process is rooted in continuous research, innovation, and hands-on experimentation. We operate as a core design studio with expertise across four main verticals: planning & Architecture, Interior Design, Landscape Design, and Design-Build solutions, complemented by a full suite of allied engineering consultancy services.

We go beyond conventional design practices by offering advanced turnkey solutions to our clients, using the latest technologies and high-performance construction materials. At AR & Associates, our mission is to enhance our clients’ lifestyle and brand presence through custom, context-sensitive design solutions. Rather than following a fixed design style, we treat every project as a blank canvas—developing it with care and creativity, drawing inspiration from the site, the narrative, and the client’s aspirations. Our projects are characterized by thoughtful detailing and a user-centric approach, with a strong focus on improving the overall spatial experience and the built environment.
